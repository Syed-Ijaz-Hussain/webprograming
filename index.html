About Our Society

Dewan Welfare Society was founded in 2010 with a mission to uplift underprivileged communities through sustainable development programs. Our organization works tirelessly to provide education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities to those in need.

With over 500 volunteers and 50+ programs across the country, we have impacted more than 10,000 lives directly and countless more indirectly through our community initiatives.
